FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our ERP and software development services.

What software development services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive software development services including custom software development, website development, application development (iOS/Android), custom accounting software, mobile billing software, and business process automation. All services include Arabic language support and GCC compliance.

Do you develop custom accounting software for GCC businesses?

Yes, we specialize in developing custom accounting software tailored for GCC businesses. Our solutions include GCC VAT compliance, Arabic invoicing templates, multi-currency support, bank integration, and localization for each GCC country's specific requirements.

What is your development process?

Our development process includes requirement analysis, design & planning, development, testing (including Arabic UI testing), deployment, and ongoing support. We follow agile methodology with regular updates and client feedback sessions.

Do you provide Arabic language support in software?

Yes, all our software solutions include full Arabic language support including interface, reports, invoices, receipts, and documentation. We also provide right-to-left (RTL) layout support and Arabic UI/UX design for optimal user experience in the Middle East.
